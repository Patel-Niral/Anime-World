description
"The Devil is a Part-Timer!" is a comedic anime that follows the demon lord Satan, who is forced to flee his world and take refuge in modern-day Tokyo after being defeated by the hero Emilia. In order to survive in the human world, Satan gets a part-time job at a fast-food restaurant called MgRonald's, where he adopts the name Sadao Maou. Alongside his loyal general, Alciel, and a quirky neighbor named Chiho, Sadao navigates the challenges of everyday life while plotting his return to power. With its blend of humor, fantasy, and slice-of-life elements, "The Devil is a Part-Timer!" offers a fresh and entertaining twist on the classic demon lord trope.
