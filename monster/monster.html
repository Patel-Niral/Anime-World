description
"Monster" is a gripping anime that follows the story of Dr. Kenzo Tenma, a brilliant neurosurgeon working in Germany. When he saves the life of a young boy named Johan Liebert instead of a prominent politician, Tenma's life takes a dark turn. As mysterious murders and conspiracies unfold, Tenma becomes entangled in a complex web of deception and intrigue. Determined to stop Johan, whom he believes to be the embodiment of evil, Tenma embarks on a perilous journey to uncover the truth behind the boy's past and his own role in shaping the events that unfold. With its psychological depth, suspenseful plot twists, and morally complex characters, "Monster" is a thought-provoking and emotionally intense anime that keeps viewers on the edge of their seats until the very end.
